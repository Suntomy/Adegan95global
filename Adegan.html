Community Orchards

Edible Flowers & Herbs

Harmonious Fauna

Project: Street Pantry (Pilot)

Convert select streets into edible corridors: shade + food + flowers. Pilots focus on low-maintenance, native fruit trees and pollinator-supporting plants.

Turn school lawns into learning orchards where children harvest, prepare and learn where food comes from.
